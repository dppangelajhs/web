Privacy Policy

Last updated: Monday, 23rd September of 2024

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the following information from users:

  1. Nickname: This is the name chosen by the user to represent themselves in the game.
  2. Average Score: The average score achieved by the player throughout the game.
  3. Rank: The player's position or rank based on their performance compared to other players.

2. How We Use the Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  1. To identify users within the game.
  2. To display scores and rankings.
  3. To enhance in-game interactions and competition features.

3. Data Storage

The collected data (nickname, average score, and rank) is securely stored in a database and is not shared with any third parties. We ensure that this data is protected and used only for the purposes mentioned above.

4. Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. The data is stored securely within Google's services, which follow industry-standard security protocols.

5. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  1. Request access to their nickname, average score, and rank stored in our database.
  2. Request the deletion of their nickname, average score, and rank.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at our social media.
